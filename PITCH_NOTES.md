## What It Actually Does (keep it simple)

- **Answers every call** — business hours and after hours, sounds like a real receptionist
- **Captures patient leads automatically** — name, phone, service needed, preferred times — logged to a live dashboard you check from your phone
- **Emergency alerts** — if someone calls with a dental emergency, it texts the doctor immediately
- **Morning summaries** — front desk gets a text every morning with overnight inquiries
- **You own the dashboard** — see every lead, filter urgent vs routine, mark as scheduled, export CSV

## Common Objections

**"We already have a receptionist."**
> "Totally — this isn't replacing them, it's covering the calls they can't get to. Missed calls after hours, when they're with a patient, lunch — those patients usually call the next office on the list. This just makes sure you catch them."

**"How much does it cost?"**
> "Setup is a one-time fee, then a small monthly. I keep it affordable for small offices — nothing like the enterprise tools. Happy to walk you through it and give you a real number based on your call volume."

**"We're not really a tech office."**
> "Neither is Hammond Dental — that's kind of the point. I handle all the setup. You just log into a simple dashboard. That's it."

**"We use [other software]."**
> "This doesn't replace anything you're using. It just handles the phone — the leads come to a dashboard you can check from your phone. No integration needed."

## Demo Flow (when you have them on the phone or in person)

1. Pull up the dashboard at your Vercel URL — show the live leads table
2. Call the demo number while they're watching — let them hear Jessica answer
3. Say "I need an appointment for a cleaning" — walk through the waitlist flow
4. Show the new row appear in the dashboard in real time
5. Do a quick emergency test — "I have severe tooth pain" — show the urgent badge
6. If they're sold: *"I can have yours up in about a week. I just need your office info and a number for the doc to get emergency texts."*

---

## Target Profile (best-fit offices)

- Small dental practices, 1–3 dentists
- Booked out 3–6 weeks (cancellation waitlist is valuable)
- No dedicated after-hours answering service
- Own their own practice (not a DSO)
- Las Vegas area for now — easier to reference Hammond Dental
